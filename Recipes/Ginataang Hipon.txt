Ingredients :
1 tbsp oil
1/4 cup leeks, sliced
1/2 kg shrimps, trimmed
1 (33 g) pack Knorr Ginataang Gulay Recipe
1 cup water
1 tsp garlic bits



Cook :
1. Get your pan nice and hot over medium heat. Pour some oil and sautè leeks until fragrant. Add shrimp next and sautè for another minute or until it turns bright red in color.

2. In goes the water and Knorr Ginataang Gulay Recipe. Mix well and bring this to a boil, then immediately lower the heat to simmer.

3. Simmer until the shrimps are cooked through. And that's our dish! Serve Ginataang Hipon with garlic bits on top and see how it will disappear right before your eyes. Yummy!
