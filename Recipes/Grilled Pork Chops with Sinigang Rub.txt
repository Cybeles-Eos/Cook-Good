Ingredients :
4 pcs pork chops (3/4 to 1 inch thickness)
1 (20 g) Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original
1 tsp ground black pepper, to taste
1/4 cup sesame oil
canola oil for grilling,as needed

Cook :
1. This recipe is so easy and quick to make. Just rub the combined seasoning of Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original and ground black pepper to both sides of the pork chops. You may also add freshly chopped herbs to add dynamic flavors to the seasoning.

2. Next, drizzle sesame oil over both sides of the pork chops, to give it that Asian flavor and aroma.

3. Get your grill nice and hot and brush it with some oil. Grill pork chops for 3 minutes per side then flip. Do this until pork chops are cooked through.

4. Serve with ranch dressing. You will definitely eat more than usual especially with a side of grilled tomatoes, onions, and mushrooms, it's a winner!
